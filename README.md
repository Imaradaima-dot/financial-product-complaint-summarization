# Financial Product Complaint Summarization

## Overview
This project applies Generative AI techniques to classify and summarize customer complaints in the financial sector. The goal is to convert unstructured complaint narratives into structured product categories and concise summaries that support faster, more effective complaint handling.

## Business Context
Customer complaints are an important signal for financial institutions because they highlight service issues, operational gaps, and potential compliance concerns. Complaints often need to be routed to the correct team based on the product area, such as credit reports, student loans, money transfers, or other financial services.

Manual review can be time-consuming, especially when complaint volumes are high. Generative AI can improve this process by classifying complaints into relevant product categories and summarizing the key grievance so support teams can quickly understand and respond to customer concerns.

## Project Objectives
- Classify customer complaints into relevant financial product categories
- Compare zero-shot and few-shot prompting for text classification
- Generate concise summaries of complaint narratives
- Support faster routing and improved complaint response workflows

## Methods Used
- Python
- Pandas
- Natural language processing
- Generative AI
- Zero-shot prompting
- Few-shot prompting
- Text-to-label classification
- Text-to-text summarization
